About

Enjoy animal attractions at our 150-acre wildlife zoo. Encounter a wide range of wildlife habitats such as giraffes, tigers, zebras, gibbons, kangaroos, lions, and much more. Enjoy a thrilling safari ride and feed some of the wildlife animals, such as, elephants, monkeys, deer, bisons, yaks, and many more.

The Bluestone Zoo is located in Seethakonda Reserve Forest. It is surrounded by scenic hills of the Eastern Ghats on three sides and a sea on the fourth. The animals are segregated according to their geographic territories for peaceful coexistence in the zoo. The flora and soil are matched with the native habitats of the animals. The zoo is recognized by the Central Zoo Authority and is home to 843 animals like mammals, carnivores, canids, ungulates, reptiles, birds, and butterflies belonging to 123 species in a natural ambiance. It also shelters many free-ranging animals and birds owing to its existence in a forest area close to the wildlife sanctuary.
